July - Sept 2019

Aug 3rd, 2019 - Annual Back to School Fair

The University of Kansas - Department of Family Medicine and Community Health partcipated in the Wyandotte County Back-to-School Fair at Kansas City Kansas Community College (KCKCC). Some photos cannot be shared here to protect the privacy of the the young patients in them.

July 30, 2019 - Brad is in town

Brad Gautney comes to Kansas City for a CATSystem meeting. We had very fruitful discussions and some nice lunch with the team together.

July 22, 2019 - Attending an AR/VR Lecture

Attended a digital health lecture on Augmented and Virtual Reality. Of one particular interest was the MERGE Cube technology which we intend to explore.

July 18, 2019 - Kevin Travels to Kansas City

Kevin Oyowe, CATSystem Programmer, travels to Kansas City, KS, United States for an exchange visitor program at The University of Kansas Medical Center.

July 3rd, 2019 - Nakuru Site Visit

The CATSystem team visited the Nakuru County Referral Hospital in the week of July 1st 2019. The purpose of this visit was a formative work to touch bases with the various stake holders at the Nakuru pilot site and lay the foundation for the upcoming CATSystem project.

Riding on the success of the HIT System sister project, the team was able to hold successful meetings with the Medical Superintendent Nakuru CRH, the Nursing Officer In-Charge, Gynecology department, as well as a general meeting with the relevant providers.
