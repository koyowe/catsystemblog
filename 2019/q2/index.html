April - June 2019

June 13, 2019 - Support for HITSystem

Supported communications between HITSystem development team and NASCOP leading to the resolution of a technical glitch with the EID website that was preventing the developers from querying the dashboard.

May 31, 2019 - Project Development Plan

The project development plan is available for download here CATSystem Project Development Plan

May 24, 2019 - Cervical Cancer Screening, Treatment, and referral documents

The following documents are available for download.

  • CATSystem - Cervical Cancer Screening and Treatment Flowchart
  • CATSystem Interview Mock Previews
  • CATSystem Offline App Presentation
  • CATSystem Project Development Plan
  • CATSystem Screening and Treatment SOP

May 17, 2019 - Cervical Cancer Research and Study

Conducted some research on Cervical Cancer Screening and Treatment. Most of the activity involved studying and reviewing publications and guidelines about cervical cancer screening and treatment in Kenya. Online publications and guidelines by the Kenya Ministry of Health and KEMRI Researchers also proved helpful.

May 9, 2019 - Site Visits

Visited Lumumba Sub County Hospital on May 6th and Kisumu County Hospital (KCH) on May 9th. Held discussions with the Clinicians in-charge and nurses. Got an overview of the procedures involved during cervical cancer screening and treatment. Also got a chance to interact briefly with the HIT System while at KCH.
