Feb 26, 2019 - Page Loading Animations

Previous tests have shown that CATSystem can work when completely offline, and it actually loads pages much faster offline than when connected to the Internet. But when you are connected to a slow 3G Internet connection, there is a period of time (usually about 4 seconds on a normal network speed) that must pass before a decision is made to serve content offline, and then afterwords the page may proceed to start loading as the user waits.

We still would not wish to loose any users during this wait, so we display some entertaining page loading animations to keep the user engaged as the page loads momentarily. Check the illustration below.

Feb 21, 2019 - Added Offline Functionality to the WebApp

This is expected to be a ground-breaking technology for the Cancer Tracking Project. The offline functionality was added through the use of Progressive Web Apps (PWA) technology. It makes the app capable of working completely offline and on a number of devices including Computers, Tablets, and Mobile phones.

In addition, the app is capable of installing itself on all these devices and will operate more like an Android or IOS native app, while on the Windows computers it is capable of running like a Windows app.

Feb 12, 2019 - The First Cervical Cancer Screening Form

Added a more real cervical cancer screening form to the system. The screening form was sourced from the Ministry of Medical Services online portal at Cervical Cancer Screening Form - K4Health. Please note that the form may not be upto date.

Feb 9, 2019 - The First Initial commit

On this day, the CATSystem online project demo page was created and launched by Kevin Oyowe as part of the CATSystem Programmer Interview Challenge. The challenge required candidates to present a rough vision of their idea as they would imagine what the system could look like.
